Computer Science And Engineering

Diploma in Computer Science and Engineering is a 3 year course commonly called the CSE. Computer Science and Engineering is the study of computational systems, which students are taught about computer technology (hardware and software).The aspirants, after completing the course will be able to get various job opportunities with an amazing salary scope. The Diploma in Computer Engineering jobs are available in various positions like Computer Programmer, Software Engineer, Software Developer, Computer Systems Engineer, etc.

Diploma in Electronics Engineering focuses on designing and testing ICs, inductors,   capacitors, and resistors.
